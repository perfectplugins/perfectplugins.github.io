Custom Crates & Keys VOL1

Four custom 3D models of crates and keys for your Hytale server. Plug and play asset pack -- just drop in and use.

Version1.2
TypeHytale Asset Pack (Models)
DependenciesNone

Installation

  1. Download the asset pack
  2. Place it in your server's mods/ folder
  3. Start the server -- models are available immediately

Included Models

4 unique crate and key 3D models with textures and icons:

  • Crate Model 1 -- Classic wooden crate with metal bands
  • Crate Model 2 -- Ornate treasure chest
  • Key Model 1 -- Classic skeleton key
  • Key Model 2 -- Ornate golden key

All models include matching inventory icons and are ready to use with PerfectLootbox or any other plugin that supports custom items.

Usage

The models register as items in the game. Give them via commands or integrate with PerfectLootbox for a complete crate system.

Works great alongside PerfectLootbox for a complete crate opening experience with animations.

FAQ

Can I use these with other plugins?

Yes. The models are standard Hytale items that any plugin can reference by their item ID.

Will there be more volumes?

VOL2 is planned with additional crate and key designs.
