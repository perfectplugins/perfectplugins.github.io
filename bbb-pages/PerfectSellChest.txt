[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTSELLCHEST?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Automatic Selling[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Items are sold automatically [B]every 30 seconds[/B]. No player interaction needed.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Price Multipliers[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]1x and 2x chest variants. Premium chests sell items at [B]double the price[/B].[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Per-Player Limits[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Configurable [B]max chests per player[/B]. Per-player overrides available.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Sell Notifications[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Players see how much they earned each cycle. [B]Toggleable[/B] per player.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Hologram Display[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Info display above each SellChest showing status and earnings.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Multi-Economy Support[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Compatible with [B]HTSkyBlock[/B], [B]EconomySystem[/B], [B]Ecotale[/B], and [B]Economy/Ryukazan[/B].[/SIZE]
