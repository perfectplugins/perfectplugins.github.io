[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTGUARD?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[SIZE=3]Protect your server with [B]7 anti-cheat detections[/B], a full [B]moderation toolkit[/B], and an [B]in-game admin panel[/B]. Ban, mute, freeze, jail, warn, vanish — everything you need to keep your community safe, in one plugin.[/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]7 Anti-Cheat Detections[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]— [B]Speed[/B] — Detects abnormal movement speed
— [B]Fly[/B] — Detects unauthorized flight
— [B]FastBreak[/B] — Impossibly fast block breaking
— [B]FastPlace[/B] — Impossibly fast block placing
— [B]Reach[/B] — Attacks beyond normal range
— [B]Nuker[/B] — Mass block destruction
— [B]AutoClick[/B] — Inhuman click rates
All thresholds are fully configurable. VL (violation level) system with auto-freeze and auto-jail.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Ban System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Permanent bans, temporary bans with duration, IP bans. All with reasons and history tracking.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Mute System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Permanent and temporary mutes. Word blacklist with auto-blocking.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Staff Tools[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]— [B]Vanish[/B] — Invisible staff mode
— [B]Freeze[/B] — Stop player movement
— [B]Jail[/B] — Teleport cheaters to configurable jail
— [B]Inventory Inspect[/B] — View any player's inventory
— [B]Teleport[/B] — TP to player or bring player to you
— [B]Warnings[/B] — Issue, view, and clear warnings
— [B]Player Notes[/B] — Private admin notes per player[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Reports & Alerts[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Players can submit reports. Staff receive real-time violation alerts. Full report management UI.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Announcements[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Title, notification, and chat announcements from the admin panel.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]12 UI pages covering players, violations, reports, settings, bans, mutes, blacklist, and more. All via [B]/pg menu[/B].[/SIZE]
[/LIST]
