[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTITEMMANAGER?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[SIZE=3]Take [B]full control[/B] over every item in your server. Override stats, crafting recipes, quality levels, block drops, entity drops, and more — all through a [B]beautiful in-game UI[/B]. No file editing, no restarts. Change a sword's damage, remove a recipe, or make mobs drop custom loot in seconds.[/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Item Stats Override[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Modify damage, armor, attributes for [B]any item[/B] in the game. Changes apply instantly.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Crafting Override[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Change or completely remove crafting recipes. Control which benches can craft which items.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Quality Override[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Set custom quality levels per item. Fine-tune your server's item economy.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Drop Rules[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]— [B]Block Drops[/B] — Customize what drops when any block is broken
— [B]Entity Drops[/B] — Customize what any mob drops on death
Full UI for creating and managing drop rules.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Weapon Tooltips[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Display [B]damage values[/B] directly in item tooltips. Toggleable in config.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Bulk Edit Mode[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Apply changes to [B]multiple items at once[/B]. Global multipliers to scale stats server-wide.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Full management panel via [B]/itemmanager menu[/B]. Item picker, stat editor, craft editor, drop rules — everything in one clean interface.[/SIZE]
[/LIST]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// COMMANDS[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[TABLE]
[TR]
[TD]/itemmanager menu[/TD]
[TD]Open the item management UI[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/itemmanager drops[/TD]
[TD]Open the drop rules UI[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/itemmanager reload[/TD]
[TD]Reload all overrides from disk[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/itemmanager setlang <code>[/TD]
[TD]Set language (EN, FR, ES, IT, DE, PT)[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/itemmanager help[/TD]
[TD]Display help[/TD]
[/TR]
[/TABLE]
