[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Custom Lootboxes[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Create [B]unlimited lootbox types[/B] with custom names, display settings, and drop pools.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Weighted Drop Pools[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Set [B]weight and quantity[/B] for each drop. Rare items get low weight, common items get high weight.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]10 Chest + 10 Key Models[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]— [B]Gold[/B]: Blue, Green, Pink, Red, Silver
— [B]Silver[/B]: Blue, Green, Pink, Red, Yellow
All with matching key variants.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Opening Animation[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Stunning [B]3D animation[/B] when players claim a lootbox.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Key System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Players need the [B]matching key[/B] in their inventory to open a lootbox. Distribute keys as rewards, drops, or sell them.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Holograms[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Customizable hologram display above placed lootboxes.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Placement Protection[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Placed lootboxes [B]can't be broken[/B] by players. Only admins can remove them.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Full management via [B]/plb menu[/B]. Create, edit drops, manage holograms, distribute keys.[/SIZE]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// COMMANDS[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[TABLE]
[TR]
[TD]/plb list[/TD]
[TD]List all lootboxes[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb claim <name>[/TD]
[TD]Claim lootbox (needs key)[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb menu[/TD]
[TD]Open admin UI[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb create <name>[/TD]
[TD]Create lootbox[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb adddrop <name> <item> [weight] [qty][/TD]
[TD]Add drop to pool[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb get <name> [qty][/TD]
[TD]Give lootbox to yourself[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb getkey <name> [qty][/TD]
[TD]Give key to yourself[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb givekey <player> <key> [qty][/TD]
[TD]Give key to player[/TD]
[/TR]
[TR]
[TD]/plb reload[/TD]
[TD]Reload config[/TD]
[/TR]
[/TABLE]
