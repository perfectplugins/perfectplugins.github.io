[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTPORTALS?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[SIZE=3]Tired of boring teleportation systems? PerfectPortals brings [B]12 stunning colored portal pads[/B] to your server — each with unique [B]particle effects[/B], [B]colored lighting[/B], and a beautiful [B]recolored stone base[/B]. Link any two portals together and let your players teleport between them with a simple [COLOR=#55FF55][B]press of [F][/B][/COLOR]. Perfect for hubs, dungeons, arenas, or any creative build.

[B]NEW in v2.0:[/B] Built-in [COLOR=#FF6B00][B]TCP reverse proxy[/B][/COLOR] — connect multiple Hytale servers behind a single public IP. Players walk through a portal and seamlessly transfer to another server. No external proxy tools needed.[/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]12 Portal Variants[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3][B]6 colors[/B] (Red, Green, Purple, Orange, Pink, Yellow) x [B]2 styles[/B] each:
— [B]Light version[/B] — emits colored light (2 block radius)
— [B]Dark version[/B] — same particles and texture, no light emission
Each portal has its own [B]recolored pad texture[/B], [B]matching particle effects[/B], and [B]unique inventory icon[/B].[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Warp System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Place two portals anywhere in the world and [B]link them together[/B] via the admin UI. Players simply press [B][F][/B] on a linked portal to instantly teleport to the other one. Links are [B]bidirectional[/B] — works both ways.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Cross-Server Transfer[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Configure any portal to [B]transfer players to another Hytale server[/B]. Set the target host, port, and an optional countdown timer. Works with direct IPs or through the built-in proxy.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]NEW — Built-in Reverse Proxy[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Run multiple Hytale servers behind [B]one public IP[/B]. The proxy hides your backend servers completely:
— Players connect to a single address — [B]backend IPs are never exposed[/B]
— Supports [B]Docker internal IPs[/B] (172.x.x.x), local networks (10.x.x.x), localhost
— Each backend gets its own [B]public port[/B] — fully reliable, zero protocol parsing
— Add [B]unlimited backends[/B] via a simple JSON config
— [B]Disabled by default[/B] — existing setups work without changes[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI Panel[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Clean [B]sidebar layout[/B] accessible via [B]/pportals[/B]:
— Paginated list of all placed portals with [B]ID, type, position, world[/B]
— [B]Click to select[/B] portal A, then portal B
— [B]Link / Unlink[/B] buttons with instant feedback
— [B]Server Config[/B] page — set host, port, countdown, label per portal
— Real-time [B]status messages[/B] for every action[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Auto-Registration[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Portals are [B]automatically registered[/B] when placed and [B]unregistered[/B] when broken. Links are cleaned up automatically. No manual setup needed.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Colored Lighting & Particles[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Light variants emit a [B]colored glow[/B] matching their portal color. Each color has its own [B]hue-shifted particle spawners[/B] — background glow, distortion, and frame sparks.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Full Persistence[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]All portal data, links, and server configs are [B]saved to JSON[/B] and survive server restarts.[/SIZE]
[/LIST]
