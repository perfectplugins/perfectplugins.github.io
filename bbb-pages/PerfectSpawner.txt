[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTSPAWNER?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[SIZE=3]Add [B]fully configurable mob spawners[/B] to your server. Each spawner has a [B]custom cage model[/B] matching its mob type, a [B]hologram display[/B], and [B]stacking support[/B]. Control radius, count, interval, max mobs — everything via the [B]admin UI[/B].[/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Custom Cage Models[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Every spawner has a unique [B]3D cage model[/B] matching its mob type. Looks great in any build.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Hologram Display[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Each spawner shows a [B]hologram above[/B] it with the mob name and current status. Updates in real-time.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Stacking[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Stack multiple spawners of the same type at one location to [B]increase spawn rate[/B]. Reduces entity count vs. placing many spawners.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Full Configuration[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Per-spawner settings:
— [B]Spawn Radius[/B] — How far from the spawner mobs spawn (default: 5)
— [B]Spawn Count[/B] — How many mobs per cycle (default: 2)
— [B]Spawn Interval[/B] — Time between cycles (default: 30s)
— [B]Max Mobs[/B] — Maximum alive mobs (default: 8)
— [B]Activation Range[/B] — Player must be within range (default: 32)[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]All Vanilla Mobs[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Works with all Hytale mobs — animals, hostile mobs, and special creatures.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Full management panel via [B]/spawner[/B]. Paginated spawner list, type config, settings editor.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Performance[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Event-driven spawning. Distance-based activation — inactive spawners use [B]zero resources[/B].[/SIZE]
[/LIST]

[HR][/HR]

[TR]

[TR]
