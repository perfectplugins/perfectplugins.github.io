[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// WHY PERFECTNPC?[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[SIZE=3]PerfectNPC brings your server world to life with [B]custom NPCs[/B] that can sell items, give quests, display dialogues, and rotate to face players. Every NPC is fully configurable through an [B]in-game admin UI[/B] — no file editing required.[/SIZE]

[HR][/HR]

[CENTER]
[SIZE=5][COLOR=#FF6B00][B]// FEATURES[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[/CENTER]

[LIST]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]4 NPC Types[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]— [B]Shopkeeper[/B] — Sells and buys items via interactive trade UI
— [B]Quest Giver[/B] — Assigns quests with objectives and rewards
— [B]Dialogue[/B] — Displays conversation text with multiple responses
— [B]Ambient[/B] — Decorative NPC, no interaction[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Shop System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Configurable item list, prices, and stock. Buy and sell in the same UI. Category tabs for large shops.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Quest System[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Multiple objective types: collect items, kill mobs, visit locations. Item and XP rewards. Per-player progress tracking.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Face-Me Rotation[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]NPCs rotate to face the nearest player automatically. Uses smart despawn/respawn for reliable rotation.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Chunk Spawning[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]NPCs auto-spawn when chunks load. No manual respawning needed after restarts.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Custom Models[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Use any Hytale model with custom scale. Nameplate display. Frozen/idle animations.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Admin UI[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Full in-game management panel via [B]/npc[/B]. Create, configure, and manage all NPCs visually.[/SIZE]
[*][SIZE=4][COLOR=#55FF55][B]Multi-language[/B][/COLOR][/SIZE]
[SIZE=3]Plugin messages in [B]5 languages[/B]: EN, FR, ES, DE, IT.[/SIZE]
[/LIST]
